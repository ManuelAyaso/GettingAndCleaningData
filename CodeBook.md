First I merged for each X_test / X_train data with subjectIds and labelIds, after having labelIds I've added label and removed labelId as a column.

After that both train and test data where merged (union) with rbind, then the data was updated with only the variables with "mean" or "std" on their names.

Finally it was applied the mean on each column grouped by subjectId and label (name of the activity) to generate the final tidy data.
